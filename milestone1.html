Milestone #1

What is the purpose of the site? What do you want the web site to accomplish? What opportunity, problem, or issue is your site addressing?
The purpose of the site will be to advertise a small animal rescue organization. The website will provide information on the rescue and display dogs that are up for adoption, attracting potential adopters.

Who is your intended audience?
The intended audience will be prospective pet owners or volunteers who are 18 years or older.

What type of content might be included in your site?
  • An "About" page with information about the organization like hours of operation and how/when the rescue was founded.
  • An "Adopt-a-Pet" page that displays information about dogs that are currently up for adoption, along with their photos.
  • A "Volunteer" page that informs how to get involved with the orginzation through volunteer work.
  • A "Photos" page with pictures of previusly adopted pets with their new families.
  • A "Contact" page that provides the organization's phone number, email address, and physical address.

List at least two related or similar sites found on the Web.
  1. Humane Society of Charlotte
  2. Golden Retriever Rescue Club of Charlotte
